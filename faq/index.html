FAQ

Straight answers, no filler.

How much does it cost?

Pricing starts with a $120 single session. If the founding pricing banner is still active, the first 20 clients can book at $90/session. There is also a $60 intro offer for the first session only, a $400 four-session package, a $200/mo membership, and a small number of scholarship spots at $40-$60.

Do you offer packages?

Yes. The 4-session package is the option we steer toward for steady momentum. It works out to about $100 per session and is meant for people who want a little structure without a long commitment.

What is the membership?

The membership is $200/mo and includes 2 sessions per month plus async check-in. It is the recurring option for people who want ongoing support. The async part is not built into the static site, so the delivery method is handled separately by the operator.

I cannot afford this. Is there help?

Sometimes. There are a few scholarship / equity spots each month at $40-$60. They are intentionally limited and not the default menu. If you need one, reach out and ask.

Do I need to believe in this?

No. Curiosity is enough. Come as a skeptic, a seeker, or somewhere in between.

What technology do I need?

A phone, tablet, or computer with a camera, a microphone, and a stable internet connection. Sessions are online.

I have never done energy work before. Is that okay?

Yes. The pace is gentle and the language is plain. You do not need prior experience.

Is this medical or mental-health treatment?

No. Reiki, readings, meditation, and intention-setting are complementary practices for relaxation and wellbeing, not a substitute for professional care. If you are in crisis in the US, call or text 988.

What about refunds or rescheduling?

Please reschedule at least 24 hours ahead whenever possible. Refunds are at the operator's discretion.

What time zone are the sessions in?

The scheduler shows your local time zone. The practice is US-based and everything happens online.

Can I book if I am on the fence?

Yes. If you are still deciding, read the service descriptions and choose the one that feels like the cleanest exhale.
